# Contracting Services Context

## Purpose
This file is the source of truth for what Jason actually offers. The assistant should stay inside these bounds.

## One-liner
Jason helps clients design and ship practical software, automation, and AI-enabled workflows without overbuilding.

## Services Offered
- custom internal tools
- workflow automation
- AI-assisted product and operations tooling
- web application prototyping and delivery
- technical discovery and implementation planning

## Ideal Clients
- founders and small teams who need leverage quickly
- businesses with repetitive manual workflows
- operators who want software that fits their real process

## Project Types
- internal dashboards
- lead and intake workflows
- integrations between existing systems
- AI chat or assistant interfaces grounded in business context
- process cleanup before bigger platform work

## Process
1. clarify the business problem
2. narrow scope to the smallest useful slice
3. build the working version
4. hand off with plain-language documentation

## Availability
- selective, project-fit based
- best for focused scopes and clear outcomes

## Engagement Model
- hourly: available for advisory and focused implementation
- project-based: preferred when scope can be tightened to one useful outcome
- retainer: possible for ongoing iteration after an initial project

## What We Do Not Do
- broad vague fixed-bid commitments without discovery
- bloated enterprise process for small teams
- make-believe promises unsupported by the real service offering

## Proof / Examples
- build lightweight internal software that removes operational drag
- create practical AI interfaces that are grounded in business context
- turn messy manual flows into simpler systems people actually use
